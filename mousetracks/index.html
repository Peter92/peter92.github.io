Mouse Tracks (WIP)

Record your gaming activity.

Track your mouse movement, clicks, and keyboard activity in artistic ways.

Download for Free Sign Up for Updates

What is it?

Track your mouse and keyboard activity separately for each game, allowing you to render high quality images in the colours of your choice.
It is designed to run quietly in the background, and has features in place to ensure the results still look interesting even after hundreds of hours.
